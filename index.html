Local Development & Sharing

Built for engineers, designed for speed. See how easy it is to spin up TaskPro locally and share it with your team.

Local Development

TaskPro uses zero complex build steps for the frontend UI. Just serve the files locally via Node, Python, or Live Server.

Terminal
npx serve .
# Serving! http://localhost:3000

Team Sharing

Terminal

vercel --prod
